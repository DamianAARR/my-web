About me


Hi, here is Damián


I have been a programming student for a few months. My goal is to become a Full Stack Developer and be able to work in a software development company.


Since I was young, I've been passionate about everything related to technology. By the time I was 18, I had built three computers from scratch. One for myself and two others for my friends.


What attracts me the most about programming is the fact of understanding how things work, something I always wonder about when I see something that catches my attention, and being able to build any application that comes to mind.


My close ones have always told me that I have a very analytical mind. If I add to that the fact that since I was young, I've tried to fix everything that broke at home, I think that's why I've decided to start studying programming.
